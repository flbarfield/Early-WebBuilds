About the Tucson Swing Dance Club

Founded in 1977 by Rose Marie Burrel, the Tucson Swing Dance Club is a volunteer based West Coast Swing dance club. Our mission is "To promote West Coast Swing dancing and to provide for it's growth and development within the community, employing the use of sound business and management decisions."

No Partner Required!
We meet every Thursday (excluding majory holidays) at Shall we Dance, 4101 E Grand Rd (North side of Grant Rd. halfway between Alvernon and Columbus). Volunteer instructors teach a beginner West Coast Swing lession from 7:00 - 7:45 and an intermediate lesson from 8:00 - 8:30; lessons are then followed by general dancing until 10:30pm.
