Dance With Us!

The Tucson Swing Dance Club meets every Thursday (excluding major holidays) at Shall We Danec, 4101 E Grand Rd.

Volunteer instructors teach a beginner lesson from 7:00pm - 7:45pm and then an intermediate lesson from 8:00 - 8:30 pm. Lessons are then followed by general social dancing until 10:30 pm.

COME AS YOU ARE! No experience or partner required!
